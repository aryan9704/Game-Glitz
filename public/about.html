Our Origin

Built by gamers,
for gamers.

GameGlitz was born in 2020 from a simple frustration: the gaming world deserved a better home. Three friends — a developer, a designer, and a community builder — decided to quit their day jobs and build the platform they'd always wished existed.

What started as a weekend side project quickly evolved into a full-scale engineering endeavor. Within six months, we had our first 10,000 users. Within a year, we crossed 1 million. Today, GameGlitz powers gaming experiences for over 50 million players across 150+ countries.

We're headquartered in San Francisco, with offices in London, Tokyo, Berlin, and Singapore. Our team of 500+ is united by one shared passion: making gaming as accessible, exciting, and inclusive as possible.

Milestones

The GameGlitz Journey

2020
Founded
Three co-founders launch GameGlitz from a SF garage with $2M seed funding. Beta goes live with 200 games and 10K early access users.
2021
Rapid Growth
Series A funding of $25M. Launched mobile apps for iOS and Android. Crossed 1 million registered users. Added community features and live events platform.
2022
Global Launch
Expanded to 50+ countries with full localization in 12 languages. Launched Glitz Plus subscription tier. Hit 10 million users milestone. Opened London and Tokyo offices.
2023
Platform Maturity
Series C funding of $180M at $1.2B valuation. Launched Developer Portal. Reached 25M users. Won 3 industry awards. Acquired indie studio Pixel Forge.
Today
The World's Gaming Home
50M+ users, 150+ countries, 10K+ games, 500+ team members. GameGlitz is now the #1 independent gaming platform globally — and we're just getting started.
What We Stand For

Mission & Values

Everything we build is guided by four core principles that define who we are and how we operate.

Innovation
We push the boundaries of what's possible in gaming. From AI-powered recommendations to real-time multiplayer infrastructure, innovation is our daily habit — not a quarterly initiative.
Community
Gaming is more powerful when shared. We invest deeply in tools and spaces that help players connect, compete, collaborate, and celebrate together — across every language and timezone.
Accessibility
Every gamer deserves access, regardless of ability, location, or budget. We build for everyone — with accessibility features, affordable pricing, and infrastructure in regions the industry ignores.
Quality
We obsess over every pixel, every millisecond, every interaction. Premium doesn't mean expensive — it means thoughtful. We hold ourselves to uncompromising standards in everything we ship.
