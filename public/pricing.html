Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Can I cancel my subscription at any time? ▾

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Glitz Plus or Glitz Ultimate subscription at any time from your Account Settings. Your subscription benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing period, after which your account will revert to the Free plan — with no data loss.

What happens to games I bought with a subscriber discount if I cancel? ▾

Any games you purchased remain yours permanently — your library is never affected by subscription status. Discounts only apply to future purchases made while your subscription is active. You keep everything you bought at the discounted price.

How does the monthly free game work? ▾

Glitz Plus and Ultimate subscribers receive one free game credit each month on their billing date. You can claim any eligible game from our rotating monthly selection in the Subscriptions section of your account. Unclaimed monthly games do not roll over to the next month.

Is the Glitz Plus free trial really free? ▾

Yes. The 7-day free trial for Glitz Plus requires no credit card information. You'll get full Plus benefits during the trial. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or stay on the Free plan — we won't charge you automatically without your consent.

Can I upgrade or downgrade between paid plans? ▾

Yes, you can switch between Glitz Plus and Glitz Ultimate at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period — you keep your current tier's benefits until then.
