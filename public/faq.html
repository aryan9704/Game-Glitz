Account & Security
Managing your profile, password, and keeping your account safe

Creating a GameGlitz account is free and takes less than 2 minutes. Visit account.html and click "Create Account." You'll need a valid email address and to choose a unique username.

You can also sign up using your Google, Discord, Steam, or Apple account for a faster registration experience — no password required for social sign-ins.

  • Use a strong, unique password (minimum 8 characters)
  • Verify your email address after registration
  • Enable two-factor authentication for maximum security

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send you a secure reset link within 2-3 minutes.

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder. Reset links expire after 24 hours for security reasons. If you've lost access to your email, contact our support team for account recovery assistance.

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on your account. Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and click "Enable."

We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password) as well as SMS verification. Authenticator apps are significantly more secure than SMS. You'll also receive backup codes to store safely in case you lose access to your 2FA device.

Yes! Free accounts can change their username once every 30 days. Glitz Plus subscribers can change their username once every 14 days. Glitz Ultimate members have no cooldown and can change freely.

Username changes take effect immediately across all GameGlitz products. Your old username will be available for others to claim 30 days after your change.

Act immediately: change your password right away from a trusted device, and revoke all active sessions via Account Settings → Security → Active Sessions → "Log Out All Devices."

Then submit a support ticket flagged as "Compromised Account." Our trust and safety team responds to these within 1 hour, 24/7. We'll help you recover any items or currency that may have been affected.

You can request account deletion from Account Settings → Settings → Danger Zone → "Delete Account." This begins a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel the deletion by logging back in.

Please note: account deletion is permanent. Your game library, achievements, and GlitzCoins will be lost and cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting your data first via Settings → Privacy → Download My Data.

Purchases & Payments
Buying games, payment methods, billing, and receipts

We accept a wide range of payment methods including all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and GlitzCoins (our virtual currency).

In select regions we also support bank transfers, Klarna (buy now, pay later), and over 80 local payment methods including UPI, PIX, Paytm, and WeChat Pay.

GameGlitz is designed with security best practices in mind. Payment card details are handled by a dedicated payment processor and are never stored on our servers. All connections are encrypted in transit.

For production deployments, a certified PCI-compliant payment processor (such as Stripe) would be integrated to handle all card transactions securely.

First, wait 10-15 minutes — delivery can sometimes be delayed. Check your Library tab and your registered email for a confirmation receipt. Ensure you're logged into the correct account.

If the issue persists after 30 minutes, contact support with your order confirmation number. We'll investigate and either deliver your purchase or issue a full refund, typically within 2 business hours.

Yes! On any game page, click "Buy as Gift" instead of the standard purchase button. You'll need the recipient's GameGlitz username or email. Gift codes can also be scheduled for delivery on a future date — perfect for birthdays.

Gift recipients have 90 days to redeem their gift. If not redeemed within 90 days, the gift code remains valid but the original buyer can contact support to extend the expiry.

Payment declines can happen for several reasons:

  • Incorrect card details (number, expiry, or CVV)
  • Insufficient funds in the account
  • Your bank blocking international/online transactions
  • Your card not being activated for online purchases
  • Fraud prevention flags by your bank

Try a different payment method or contact your bank. If declines persist, reach out to our payment support team.

Pricing varies by region. In the European Union, prices shown include VAT. In the United States, applicable state sales tax is calculated and displayed at checkout. In other regions, tax is applied per local regulations.

Your receipt will always clearly show the base price and any applicable taxes separately. If you believe you've been incorrectly taxed, contact support with your order number.

Subscriptions
Glitz Plus, Glitz Ultimate, billing cycles, and plan management

Glitz Plus ($9.99/mo): Access to 200+ free-to-play games, 10% discount on all purchases, exclusive profile cosmetics, ad-free browsing, and priority customer support.

Glitz Ultimate ($19.99/mo): Everything in Glitz Plus plus access to the full game vault (500+ titles), 20% discount, early access to new releases, exclusive tournament entry, dedicated account manager, and unlimited cloud saves.

You can cancel anytime from Account Settings → Subscriptions → Manage Plan → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain full benefits until then.

We don't do dark patterns here. The cancel button is clearly visible and works immediately. No hoops to jump through.

Yes! Glitz Plus and Glitz Ultimate subscribers can pause their subscription for up to 3 months per year. Go to Account Settings → Subscriptions → Pause Subscription and choose your pause duration (1, 2, or 3 months).

During a pause, you won't be billed and your subscription benefits are temporarily suspended. Your game library and all progress are preserved. The subscription automatically resumes at the end of your pause period.

New GameGlitz accounts are eligible for a 7-day free trial of Glitz Plus. Glitz Ultimate free trials (also 7 days) are available during promotional periods or when referred by an existing Ultimate member.

Trials require a valid payment method on file but you won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before the trial ends with no charge. Trial availability may vary by region.

Glitz Ultimate includes a Family Sharing feature allowing up to 5 family members (same household) to benefit from vault access and discounts. Each member must have their own GameGlitz account.

Glitz Plus is for individual use only. Family sharing must be configured from Account Settings → Family → Add Member. Members under 18 require parental approval.

Technical Support
Download issues, performance, bugs, and platform compatibility

GameGlitz is available on Windows (10/11), macOS (Ventura and later), iOS (16+), and Android (10+). Our web app works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

A Linux desktop client (Ubuntu/Debian) is currently in beta. Console support (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch) is in active development with announcements expected in 2025.

Try these steps in order:

  • Restart the GameGlitz desktop client
  • Verify game files: right-click the game in your library → "Verify Files"
  • Update your graphics drivers to the latest version
  • Check that your system meets the game's minimum requirements
  • Temporarily disable antivirus/firewall software
  • Reinstall the game if all else fails

If the game still won't launch, submit a bug report with your system info and we'll investigate within 24 hours.

GameGlitz uses a global CDN with servers in 40+ locations. If speeds are low:

  • Try changing your download region in Settings → Downloads → Server Region
  • Check if download throttling is enabled (Settings → Downloads → Bandwidth Limit)
  • Pause and resume the download to reconnect to a faster server
  • Check your internet connection with a speed test at speedtest.net
  • Downloads may be slower during peak hours (evenings in your region)

Use the in-app bug reporter (F12 shortcut on desktop) for quick reports with automatic system diagnostics attached. For detailed issues, visit support.html and select "Bug Report."

Include your operating system, GameGlitz version, the specific steps to reproduce the bug, and any error messages. Screenshots or screen recordings are extremely helpful. Verified bugs are typically addressed within 72 hours.

Free accounts get 1GB of cloud save storage. Glitz Plus subscribers get 10GB, and Glitz Ultimate subscribers get unlimited cloud saves. Cloud sync happens automatically when you exit a game.

You can view, manage, and restore save files from Account Settings → Cloud Saves. Saves are retained for 6 months after account deletion (excluding permanent deletion requests).

First, update to the latest version of the app — most crash issues are fixed in updates. Then try clearing the app cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache on mobile; or reinstalling on desktop).

If crashes continue, check our system status page to see if there's an ongoing incident. You can also share crash logs from Settings → Help → Send Crash Report for our engineers to investigate.

Community Guidelines
Rules, bans, appeals, and community standards

GameGlitz is committed to being a safe, welcoming space for all players. Prohibited behavior includes:

  • Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, or disability
  • Cheating, hacking, or using unauthorized third-party software in games
  • Sharing another person's private information (doxxing)
  • Spamming, scamming, or phishing attempts
  • Selling or trading accounts
  • Distributing illegal or harmful content

See our full Community Guidelines for complete details.

Click the three-dot menu on any user profile or message and select "Report." Choose the reason that best describes the violation and optionally add details. Reports are anonymous — the reported user won't know who filed them.

For urgent safety concerns (threats of violence, CSAM), use the "Urgent Safety Report" option which goes to a human moderator within 15 minutes, 24/7.

If you believe your ban was issued in error, submit an appeal within 30 days at support.html → "Account Appeal." Include your username, the date of ban, and a detailed explanation of why you believe the ban was unjust.

Our appeals team reviews all submissions within 5 business days. Bans issued for severe violations (illegal content, doxxing) are not eligible for appeal.

GameGlitz uses GlitzShield, our proprietary anti-cheat system, which runs alongside supported games to detect unauthorized software, memory manipulation, and statistical anomalies.

First-time offenders receive a 30-day competitive ban and loss of tournament earnings from affected matches. Repeat offenders receive permanent platform bans. We also coordinate with game developers to enforce their own anti-cheat policies.

GameGlitz requires users to be at least 13 years old (or 16 in the EU under GDPR). Users under 18 require parental consent for purchasing and are automatically enrolled in a curated content filter that limits mature-rated games.

Parents can manage child accounts and set spending limits, screen time limits, and content restrictions from Account Settings → Family → Child Controls.

To block a user, go to their profile → three-dot menu → "Block [Username]." Blocked users cannot message you, view your profile, or join your game sessions. You can manage blocked users from Settings → Privacy → Blocked Users.

Muting silences a user's messages in community spaces without blocking them entirely. Both features are available from any profile or chat interface.

Refunds
Refund eligibility, timelines, and how to request

We offer a 14-day, no-questions-asked refund policy for game purchases where you have played fewer than 2 hours. This is one of the most generous policies in the industry.

Additional refunds may be granted at our discretion for technical issues, fraudulent charges, or exceptional circumstances. See our full Refund Policy for complete terms.

To request a refund, submit a ticket via the Support page. Include your order details and reason for the refund. Our team will review your request and respond by email.

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. We recommend reaching out as soon as possible after purchase, and within the timeframe specified in our Refund Policy.

Once approved, refunds typically appear on your statement within 3-5 business days for credit/debit cards. PayPal refunds appear within 2-3 business days. GlitzCoins refunds are instant.

Processing times can vary based on your bank or payment provider. If you haven't received your refund after 7 business days, contact your bank and then reach out to our support team.

Subscription refunds are handled case-by-case. We offer refunds for accidental renewals if you contact us within 48 hours and haven't used the subscription benefits during that billing cycle.

We don't offer pro-rated refunds for partial months but we encourage you to cancel before your next billing date. To avoid unwanted renewals, you'll receive an email reminder 7 days before each renewal.

If your refund was denied and you believe this was an error, escalate by replying to the refund decision email or opening a new ticket citing your original refund request number.

Escalated cases are reviewed by a senior support agent. If you're still unsatisfied after escalation, you have the right to dispute the charge with your bank or payment provider, and we'll cooperate fully with their investigation.
